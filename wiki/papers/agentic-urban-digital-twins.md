## Summary

This article frames agentic urban digital twins as a new urban science direction built around human-AI co-learning agents.

## Method Snapshot

The work synthesizes urban informatics, digital twins, and agentic AI. Its role in the atlas is conceptual: it explains how autonomous or semi-autonomous agents can support urban reasoning without removing human judgment from the loop.

## Impact

This output broadens the atlas beyond disaster response. It connects Yifan's GeoAI work to the larger question of how cities can use AI agents as infrastructure for planning, simulation, and public decision support.
