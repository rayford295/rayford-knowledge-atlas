## Summary

This article presents a COVID-19 public opinion and emotion monitoring system based on time series thermal new word mining.

## Method Snapshot

The work analyzes public language signals during a health crisis. It is an early collaborative output that connects text mining, public attention, and crisis-response intelligence.

## Impact

This output matters because it gives the atlas a public-signal lineage before the later spatial and multimodal disaster work. It shows an early interest in how data systems can read social conditions during emergencies.
