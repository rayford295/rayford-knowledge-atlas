## Core Question

Disaster AI should not only predict damage; it should explain what kind of evidence supports the claim and who can inspect that evidence.

## Why This Question Matters

This question turns reading about institutions, pressure, leadership, and public trust into a research criterion. It asks every disaster model to show its trail: image, text, location, uncertainty, and human review.

## Reading Inputs

War, institutional, and narrative readings help clarify why evidence fails when organizations face pressure.

## Research Outputs

DamageArbiter, Hyperlocal Disaster Damage Assessment, and DisasterVLP each answer part of this question through street-view imagery, semantic categories, and multimodal arbitration.
