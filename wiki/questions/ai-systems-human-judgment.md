## Core Question

The important question is not whether AI can make a prediction. The harder question is how AI helps people judge, challenge, and reuse that prediction.

## Why This Question Matters

Many readings on AI, biographies, and public technology narratives ask what kind of human agency survives automation. This bridge keeps that concern visible inside the research graph.

## Reading Inputs

AI futures, founder biographies, and technology histories feed this question.
